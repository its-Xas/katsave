What We Are

Katsave Sustain Planet International (KASPI) is a nonprofit born from a lifelong passion for nature, inspired by the melodies of birds, unique trees, bees, rocks, and hot springs. Founded by Dr. Kamugisha Marsiale, its roots trace back to a childhood connection with the natural world, deepened by environmental studies in Uganda and Costa Rica.

Guided by the Earth Charter and Laudato Si', KASPI tackles climate change impacts like droughts and biodiversity loss, promoting pragmatic solutions and amplifying African youth voices for a sustainable future.

What KASPI Stands For

K – Knowledge & Innovation: Equip youth with eco-skills.
A – Awareness & Action: Inspire ecological care.
S – Sustainability & Solutions: Promote lasting solutions.
P – Partnerships & Participation: Involve all voices.
I – Inclusion & Integrity: Uphold diversity and ethics.

Core Objectives & Activities

Conserve Nature

Plant 20,000 trees and establish 5 green spaces annually.

Agroforestry

Distribute 20,000 seedlings and host 2 farmer schools yearly.

Regenerative Agriculture

Train farmers in composting and conduct 12 coffee farm visits.
