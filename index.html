What We Are

Katsave Sustain Planet International (KASPI) is a nonprofit organization born from a long journey of inspiration and reflection on the complex relationship between humans and nature. Its roots trace back to the founder‚Äôs early childhood, shaped by a deep admiration for nature‚Äîthe melodies of birds, intricate designs of unique trees, bees, rocks, and hot springs‚Äînurturing a profound connection to the natural world.

Exposure to the Earth Charter and Pope Francis‚Äôs encyclical Laudato Si‚Äô deepened KASPI‚Äôs commitment to ecological stewardship, justice, and spiritual-ecological renewal.

KASPI was founded to tackle urgent environmental challenges such as deforestation, biodiversity loss, climate change, and resource-driven poverty that threaten food security, health, and livelihoods in vulnerable African communities. Through tree planting, community training, research, education, and policy advocacy, KASPI promotes sustainable livelihoods, climate adaptation, and responsible stewardship‚Äîstriving to build resilient communities that live in harmony with nature.

We are guided by African indigenous wisdom, the Earth Charter, and Laudato Si‚Äô, integrating environmental ethics, spirituality, and innovation into practical action for a sustainable planet.

Our Philosophy

KASPI believes that humanity and nature are inseparable partners in sustaining life on Earth. Our philosophy is rooted in the understanding that environmental stewardship, social equity, and spiritual-ecological consciousness are fundamental to a thriving and resilient planet.

Guided by African indigenous wisdom, the Earth Charter, and Laudato Si‚Äô, we affirm that sustainable development is achieved not only through innovation and technology, but also through education, ethical leadership, community empowerment, and respect for nature.

We believe that every human being‚Äîespecially youth and women‚Äîhas a vital role to play in preserving ecological balance, promoting justice, and fostering a harmonious coexistence with the Earth. This philosophy inspires transformative action, resilience, and creativity to meet present and future environmental, social, and economic challenges‚Äîstarting from Africa and extending globally.
